# About

EHEPS International Organization is an independent nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing education, humanitarian assistance, environmental protection, artificial intelligence for social good, scientific research, innovation, digital transformation, and sustainable community development.

We collaborate with communities, governments, educational institutions, nonprofit organizations, research institutions, and technology partners to develop innovative solutions that improve lives and create lasting social impact.

EHEPS is building its global technology infrastructure using Google technologies and modern cloud platforms to support education, humanitarian response, nonprofit management, AI research, and digital public services.

---

# Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, humanitarian assistance, environmental stewardship, responsible artificial intelligence, scientific research, innovation, digital inclusion, and sustainable development.

---

# Vision

To become a globally recognized nonprofit organization advancing education, humanitarian development, environmental sustainability, ethical artificial intelligence, and digital innovation for future generations.
